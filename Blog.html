Success Story

Smart Lab Optimization through AI.

From underutilized las to Optimized utilization

Situation: A diagnostics company operated multiple labs across the city, where over 50% of lab capacity was underutilized while others were overburdened; since each diagnostic machine has a limited lifespan, this imbalance meant the expected ROI was not being achieved.
Task: The goal was to maximize utilization of lab machinery by identifying capacity gaps, understanding demand patterns, and optimizing patient distribution across locations.
Action: We used AI-driven forecasting to predict demand and machine availability, identified underutilized labs, and redirected patient flow through smart scheduling and targeted incentives.
Result: This led to a 30–40% increase in overall lab utilization and a significant improvement in operational efficiency (25%+), along with faster turnaround times, better asset ROI, and an enhanced patient experience.
